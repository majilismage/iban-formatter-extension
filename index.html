Getting Started with IBANify

Welcome to IBANify! Here's how to get the most out of your extension:

Using the Context Menu

  1. Find an IBAN on any webpage
  2. Select the text containing the IBAN
  3. Right-click and select "Format IBAN"
  4. The formatted IBAN will be copied to your clipboard

Using the Popup Interface

  1. Click the IBANify icon in your browser toolbar
  2. Enter or paste an IBAN into the text field
  3. View validation results and formatting options
  4. Click "Copy" next to your preferred format

Frequently Asked Questions

What countries are supported?

IBANify supports IBAN formats from over 70 countries worldwide, including all EU member states, the UK, Switzerland, and many more.

Does IBANify collect my data?

No. IBANify processes everything locally on your device and does not collect, store, or transmit any data.

Why does IBANify need webpage access?

IBANify needs access to page content to detect and format IBANs you select on webpages. No content is ever sent anywhere.

Troubleshooting

Context Menu Option Not Appearing

If the "Format IBAN" option doesn't appear in your right-click menu:

  • Make sure you've selected text before right-clicking
  • Try refreshing the page
  • Check if the extension is enabled in Chrome's extensions menu

Invalid IBAN Message

If you're seeing an "Invalid IBAN" message:

  • Check for typos in the IBAN
  • Ensure the IBAN follows the correct format for its country
  • Verify the check digits are correct
